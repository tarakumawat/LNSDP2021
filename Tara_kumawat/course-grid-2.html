About US

GOVT GIRLS SEN.SEC.BAGRU was established in 1961 and it is managed by the Department of Education. It is located in Urban area. It is located in SANGANER block of JAIPUR district of Rajasthan. The school consists of Grades from 6 to 12. The school is Girls and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section.
